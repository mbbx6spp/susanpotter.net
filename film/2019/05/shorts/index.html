Written by Susan Potter with ♥ on 11 May 2019 in 1 min

My absolute favorite format of film is the short film. Here are a list of short films I loved watching:

  • Re-Gifted (2018): CGI 3D short film that follows the life of a decrative ornament.
  • 3x3 (2009): A tale of two different approaches to mastery.
  • The Bigger Picture (2015): Two brothers struggling to cope with their elderly mother's care.
  • Round About Five (2005): A bicycle courier and a businessman share a fleeeting moment during a London rush hour.
  • Restoration (2018): A man drives his father's restored car and contemplates the gravity of the past.
